Wallbox board and CEO buy $5M in unison as Uber puts $20M into Lime

A clean EV-charging insider cluster leads a quiet holiday tape, with two fresh biotech 13Ds and a strategic corporate accumulator scaling up.

No watchlist-whale 13Ds, early 13Fs, or Scion filings crossed the wire. The tape today is carried by a clean EV-charging insider cluster and a strategic corporate accumulator.

Fresh 13Ds

  • CALC — Bering Partners II GP filed a new SCHEDULE 13D on CalciMedica, an immunology-focused biotech. A first-time activist-scale stake in a small-cap developer is worth a flag. Filing
  • PASG — Lynx1 Capital Management opened a 13D on Passage BIO, a gene-therapy name that has spent the year near cash value. Concentrated biotech money stepping in at the bottom of the range. Filing

Skipped as internal/affiliated plumbing: Gould Investors' twin 13Ds on BRT Apartments and OLP (One Liberty), the Jefferies-to-Jefferies-BDC filing, and the Hugreat/Alpex SPAC stake. On the amendment side, LFCR (Lifecore Biomedical) drew same-day 13D/A activity from both 22NW and Legion Partners — the seat-holders are still active, worth watching, but no fresh escalation disclosed.

Insider tape — clusters

  • WBX (Wallbox N.V.) — the standout. Four insiders bought ~$5.0M of the EV-charging maker on the same day: three directors at $1.36M each plus CEO Enric Asuncion Escorsa adding $887k. Board-plus-CEO buying in unison, near multi-year lows, is the highest-conviction print on the tape.
  • CREX (Creative Realities) — CEO Richard Mills bought $700k and CFO Tamra Koshewa $100k of the digital-signage micro-cap, an $800k C-suite cluster at $3.50.
  • PSNY (Polestar) — three directors added a combined ~$78k. Small, but directional board buying in the EV maker.

Insider tape — notable singles

  • LIME (Neutron Holdings) — Uber put $20M to work (800k shares at $25) lifting its stake in the micromobility operator. A strategic corporate buyer scaling up is a different signal than an insider, but the size demands notice.
  • BNR (Burning Rock Biotech) — director Yusheng Han disclosed $6.2M of open-market buying across the June 11 session in the China cancer-diagnostics name.
  • HDSN (Hudson Technologies) — Hartree Partners, a 10% owner, added $4.5M of the refrigerant reclaimer at ~$5.85.
  • BBD (Bank Bradesco) — CLO/executive officer Julio Cesar Bueno bought $2.0M of the Brazilian bank.
  • PSBD (Palmer Square Capital BDC) — 10% owner Martin Bicknell added $845k near $10.55.
